# 📊 Electronics Sales Dashboard

This interactive **Excel Dashboard** offers a comprehensive overview of **product sales performance**, **customer loyalty analysis**, and **sales trends** for electronic products across the years 2023 and 2024. It allows easy filtering and exploration of key metrics by product type and add-ons purchased.

---

## 🛒 Dashboard Overview

### 💰 Key Metrics
- **Total Sales Revenue**: ₹52,48,102.57
- **Total Quantity Sold**: ₹1,999.00
- **Average Unit Price**: ₹476.49

## 📈 Visualizations

### 1. **Total Price Distribution (Pie Chart)**
- Breaks down total revenue by product type and loyalty segmentation.

### 2. **Add-on Revenue Analysis (Area Chart)**
- Highlights product-type-wise revenue from add-ons.

### 3. **Top 5 Products by Loyalty Score (Column Chart)**
- Visualizes count and sum of loyalty scores across product types.

### 4. **Sales Trend of Top 3 Products (Line Graph)**
- Year-wise comparison of total price for Laptops, Smartphones, and Smartwatches.

### 5. **Total Price by Product (Bar Chart)**
- Shows the individual and cumulative contributions of each product category.
